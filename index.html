About ITD Day

The Information Technology Department Day 2026 celebrates innovation, creativity, teamwork, and technology. Students participate in exciting activities that showcase their technical skills, talents, sportsmanship, and passion for Information Technology.

This year's theme, "Back to Bits: Connecting Minds from Pixels to Possibilities", highlights how technology connects ideas, people, and opportunities while promoting collaboration, leadership, and excellence among students.
